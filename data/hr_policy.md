# Northstar Analytics HR Policy Handbook
 
Last updated: 2025-09-15
 
## Paid Time Off
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off per calendar year. PTO accrues monthly and unused PTO may be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.
 
Employees must submit PTO requests at least 10 business days in advance for vacations longer than 3 consecutive business days.
 
## Sick Leave
Employees receive 8 paid sick days per calendar year. Sick leave does not carry over into the next year.
 
A medical certificate may be requested for absences longer than 3 consecutive business days.
 
## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require approval from both the department head and HR.
 
## Parental Leave
Primary caregivers are eligible for 16 weeks of paid parental leave after 12 months of continuous employment. Secondary caregivers are eligible for 6 weeks of paid parental leave.
 
## Learning Budget
Each employee has an annual learning and development budget of USD 1,500. The budget can be used for courses, conferences, certifications, and books related to the employee's role.
 
Expenses above USD 500 require manager approval before purchase.
